# Tone of Voice (example — replace via /voice-setup)

**Adjectives:** direct, concrete, a little contrarian
**Formality:** professional but human; no jargon
**Signature moves:** short staccato lines; one idea per line; specific numbers over adjectives
**Avoid:** corporate filler ("leverage", "synergy", "game-changer"), hype, exclamation marks
**Calibration line:** "Your pipeline problem isn't traffic. It's positioning."

## Do
- Lead with a sharp claim.
- Use real numbers and concrete nouns.
- Keep lines tight.

## Don't
- Hedge or pad.
- Stack adjectives.
- Use clichés.
